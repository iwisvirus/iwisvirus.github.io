If you want to know everything about everyone, here's some tools for you. 


**[User scanner](https://github.com/kaifcodec/user-scanner)**

- more accurate than holehe
- search for emails(-e), usernames(-u)

**[Sherlock](https://github.com/sherlock-project/sherlock)**

- search for usernames across 400+social network
![PDF Info Screenshot](/images/dig1.png)

**[holehe](https://github.com/megadose/holehe)**

- search for registered accounts over  120 sites using emails
- doesn't alarm the target 
- a bit of a downside for this one is that there are a lot of websites that are rate limited which will not allow you to not even search if you have an account there or no
![PDF Info Screenshot](/images/dig2.png)

**[inteltechniques](https://inteltechniques.com/tools/index.html)**

- you can search something for example usernames, names, addresses, phone numbers, images etc. Then you can click on the button and it'll take you to that link with the whatever you searched for

**[epieos](https://epieos.com/)**

- You can reverse lookup through email and phone numbers

**[hunchly](https://hunch.ly/)**

- for invesigative professionals, online researchers, and anyone who needs unbiased fact-finding with no room for error

**[overpass turbo](https://overpass-turbo.eu/)**

- detailed information about maps and locations etc

**[pacer](https://pacer.uscourts.gov/)**

- court records

This is the [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zhTjzscNLEI) link if you want to listen a thorough explanation on each website. 

These are some useful tools when you are trying to search people up during an investigation. But also I find it very scary. Should always be careful of what you do or post on the internet.
