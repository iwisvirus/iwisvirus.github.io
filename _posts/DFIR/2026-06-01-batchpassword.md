**Batch Pasdsword Recovery**
- allows applying same pw recovery settings to multiple encrypted files disk images and partitions
- reduces downtown over evenings and weekends
- groups encrypted files and disk images in order to 
  - customize pw recovery settings for multiple encrypted items
  - prioritize encrypted files/disk images by importance
  - prioritize encrypted files/disk images by decryption complexity
