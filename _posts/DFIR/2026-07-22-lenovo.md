Today, I was trying to decrypt Thinkpad Lenovo laptop's bitlocker that we don't know the password for. 
I used passware kit ultimate and followed the steps. I had to use a usb stick to boot up passware kit inside, 
then connect it to the laptop. I had a hard time booting it up cause it kept giving me a secure boot violation error message about 
not being able to detect the kit. I tried pressing F12(Boot Menu) and F1(BIOS/UEFI setup) multiple times and it kept giving me the same 
message. So I tried something else. I did a full shutdown (holding SHIFT while clicking shutdown) 
and tapped F! repeatedly the instant I hit the power button. 

It finally worked! I went into the BIOS setup and disabled the secure boot option. Then, I restarted the computer
which allowed the passware kit to download the bin from the laptop. I uploaded that bin file on passware
and guess what... The device was not supported. Very sad. 

Well, I guess I learned how to decrypt a bitlocker key. The troubleshooting process was very enjoyable.
